Golf Places in Laos

Explore Laos' finest golf courses and destinations

Luang Prabang Golf Club

Luang Prabang, Laos
18 Holes 7,200 yards

Set against stunning mountain backdrops, this championship course offers a challenging yet scenic experience designed to blend with the natural landscape.

Long Vien Golf Club

Vientiane, Laos
18 Holes 6,850 yards

One of Vientiane's premier courses featuring rolling fairways, strategic bunkering, and scenic water features. Known for excellent conditioning.

SEA Games Golf Club

Vientiane, Laos
27 Holes 7,100 yards

Built for the SEA Games, this course offers a challenging layout with wide fairways and large greens. Host to numerous international tournaments.

Lakeview Golf Club

Vientiane, Laos
18 Holes 6,900 yards

Scenic course with beautiful lake views on multiple holes. Offers a relaxing golf experience with well-maintained fairways and friendly staff.

Mekong Golf & Resort

Vientiane Province, Laos
18 Holes 7,050 yards

A championship course situated along the Mekong River featuring challenging holes, water hazards, and a full-service resort with accommodations.

Lao Country Club

Vientiane, Laos
18 Holes 6,800 yards

Traditional country club atmosphere with a well-designed course suitable for all skill levels. Known for its excellent practice facilities.
