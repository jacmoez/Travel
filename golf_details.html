Luang Prabang Golf Escape

3 days · 2 nights · 1 championship round

Back to packages

DAY 1: ARRIVE LUANG PRABANG

Arrive at Luang Prabang International Airport. Private transfer to your boutique hotel. After check‑in, enjoy a leisurely afternoon exploring the UNESCO town – visit the Royal Palace Museum and Wat Xieng Thong. Evening welcome dinner at a traditional Lao restaurant overlooking the Mekong.

Meal: Dinner
Overnight: Luang Prabang (4-star hotel)

DAY 2: GOLF AT LUANG PRABANG GOLF CLUB

Morning transfer to Luang Prabang Golf Club (18 holes, par 72). Enjoy a scenic round surrounded by mountains and tropical forest. Shared cart and caddie included. After golf, return to town for a relaxing afternoon. Optional sunset cruise on the Mekong (supplement applies).

Meal: Breakfast, Lunch (clubhouse)
Overnight: Luang Prabang

DAY 3: KUANG SI FALLS & DEPARTURE

Morning excursion to the stunning Kuang Si Falls – swim in turquoise pools and visit the bear rescue centre. Return to town for lunch, then transfer to the airport for your departure flight.

Meal: Breakfast, Lunch
