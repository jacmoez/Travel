PACKAGE 2: 4 DAYS 3 NIGHTS VIENTIANE – LUANG PRABANG

DAY 1: Overnight in Vientiane With Guide (Lunch)

Upon arrival at Wattay International Airport, meet your guide and begin a city tour of Vientiane. Visit major landmarks including:

  • That Luang Stupa (national symbol)
  • Wat Sisaket
  • Haw Phra Keo Museum

After lunch, continue to Patuxai Monument and enjoy views of the Mekong River. In the evening, visit the night market.

Meal: Lunch
Overnight: Vientiane

DAY 2: Vientiane to Luangprabang Half Day City Tour in LPQ

After breakfast, transfer to the train station and travel to Luang Prabang. Upon arrival, visit key temples including Wat Visoun, Wat Xieng Thong, and Wat Sens. Enjoy lunch at a local restaurant.

In the afternoon, visit the National Museum and enjoy a coffee break at a heritage house. Evening at leisure with night market visit.

Meal: Breakfast, Lunch
Overnight: Luang Prabang

DAY 3: Luang Prabang Ancient City Tour Protected by UNESCO (B/L/D)

Begin with alms giving and morning market visit. Continue to Kuang Si Waterfall and Pak Ou Caves. Enjoy lunch during the excursion.

In the evening, explore Mount Phousi and the night market.

Meal: Breakfast, Lunch
Overnight: Luang Prabang

DAY 4: Departure to Vientiane and Drop at Airport (B/L)

After breakfast, check out and transfer to the train station. Arrive in Vientiane, enjoy lunch, and transfer to the airport for departure.

Meal: Breakfast, Lunch
