Luang Prabang

Luang Prabang Province, Northern Laos

About this location

Luang Prabang is a UNESCO World Heritage Site located in northern Laos at the confluence of the Mekong and Nam Khan rivers. It is renowned for its well-preserved architectural, religious, and cultural heritage, blending traditional Lao and European colonial styles.

The town is known for its numerous Buddhist temples and monasteries, daily morning alms giving ceremony, and vibrant night market. Surrounded by mountains and waterfalls, it serves as a cultural and spiritual center of Laos.
